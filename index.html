Speak Minion or Banana!

But what exactly is minion. Minions are fictional characters that appear in the Despicable Me franchise, which started with Despicable Me (2010). They are characterized by their childlike behavior and unique language, which is almost intelligible at times. So this app is created to translate english language to minion. Enjoy!
